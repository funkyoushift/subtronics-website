Gaming PC FAQ

Should I buy a prebuilt or a custom gaming PC?

If you are focused on the lowest possible budget, a strong prebuilt plus Subtronics setup can be the best value. Once budgets climb, custom builds become more attractive because the part quality and upgrade path improve.

What is your lowest custom gaming PC tier?

Our starter custom tier begins with the Ryzen 5 7600X bundle and starts around $1,299, with a 7600X3D upgrade available in the same tier.

Can you move my files to a new gaming PC?

Yes. We can migrate data, clean up software, optimize the system, and help make the transition from the old computer easier.

Not sure which path makes the most sense?

Tell us your budget, the games you play, and whether you care more about value or customization. We will point you toward the smartest option.
