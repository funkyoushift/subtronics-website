TechCare Membership Agreement

Terms and conditions for Subtronics TechCare.

1. Overview

Subtronics TechCare (“TechCare”) is a membership program offered by Subtronics LLC that provides remote support, maintenance, and discounted repair services.

2. Membership & Billing

  • Membership is billed monthly or annually depending on the plan selected at checkout.
  • Membership renews automatically unless canceled.
  • Prices and included benefits may be updated with notice.

3. Included Services

Included services may include remote support, malware removal, tune-ups, diagnostics, priority scheduling, and labor discounts as described on the TechCare page.

Parts, hardware components, and third-party subscriptions/licenses are not included unless explicitly stated in writing.

4. Services Not Included

  • Hardware parts and replacement components
  • Advanced data recovery / clean-room recovery
  • On-site service outside the service area
  • Commercial IT management / server administration (unless separately contracted)

5. Customer Responsibilities

  • You are responsible for maintaining backups of important files and data.
  • You must provide accurate information about your device and issue.
  • You confirm you own the device or have permission to authorize service.

6. Data Loss Disclaimer

Subtronics LLC is not responsible for data loss, corruption, or inaccessible data that may occur due to existing device issues, hardware failure, malware, software updates, or during diagnostics and repair. If data is critical, you should maintain backups prior to service.

7. 90-Day Workmanship Guarantee

Workmanship performed by Subtronics LLC is covered by a 90-day workmanship guarantee. This does not cover manufacturer defects, failing hardware, third-party software issues, or customer-caused damage.

8. Limitation of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by law, Subtronics LLC’s total liability related to TechCare is limited to the total amount paid for membership fees in the previous twelve (12) months.

9. Service Availability & Fair Use

TechCare is intended for reasonable personal use. Support response times may vary based on workload and scheduling. Excessive or abusive use may require additional fees or a different service plan.

10. Right to Refuse or Terminate Service

Subtronics LLC may refuse or terminate service for abusive behavior, illegal activity, fraud, or misuse of services.
