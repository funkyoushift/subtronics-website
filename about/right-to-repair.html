About Subtronics

Right to Repair

Subtronics LLC supports a practical, common-sense right-to-repair approach: people should be able to understand their devices, replace sensible parts, maintain their systems, and choose independent repair without being pushed into a full replacement too early.

What that means here

We explain things plainly

If a repair is worth doing, we will say why. If it is not, we will say that too.

We support smart DIY

That is why the site now includes troubleshooting pages and video-backed tutorial pages for common safe jobs.

We are still here for the hard part

When a job crosses into data risk, deeper diagnosis, or hardware uncertainty, we can step in and help.
