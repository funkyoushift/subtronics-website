About Subtronics LLC

Right to Repair

We believe people should have access to the knowledge and tools needed to maintain, upgrade, and understand their own computers whenever it is safe and practical to do so.

What that means here

Subtronics LLC is happy to perform repairs, upgrades, setups, and custom builds — but we also want willing people to feel more confident asking questions, trying basic maintenance, and learning how their systems work.

That is one reason this site now includes troubleshooting pages, Q&A content, and build-with-me service ideas. Teaching creates trust, and informed customers make better choices.

Safe DIY-friendly resources

  • iFixit repair guides
  • YouTube: how to install an SSD
  • YouTube: how to install RAM in a desktop PC
  • YouTube: how to clean dust from a PC

For anything involving data risk, liquid damage, burning smells, electrical concerns, or expensive hardware, professional help is usually the safer move.

What is next

This page also supports a future direction for guided PC build sessions and community learning events where people can get hands-on with computers in a lower-pressure environment.
