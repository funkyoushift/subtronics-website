Signs Your Hard Drive May Be Failing

Recognize early signs of HDD/SSD failure and learn what to do to protect your data. Local repair and recovery options available.

Common Causes

  • Aging drive hardware
  • Power loss or surges
  • Physical shock (drops/impacts)
  • Heat and poor airflow
  • Bad sectors or controller failure

Things You Can Try First

  • Back up important files immediately
  • Stop heavy use (don’t keep forcing reboots)
  • Check SMART/drive health tools
  • Avoid running repeated “repairs” if the drive is clicking
  • Prepare for a clone to a new drive

When a Repair Technician Is Needed

If the problem continues after basic troubleshooting, it may need professional diagnostics and repair:

  • Drive cloning to SSD
  • Data recovery options
  • Drive replacement and OS migration
  • Health testing and verification
