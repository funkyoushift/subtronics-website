My computer is slow — what’s the first thing to check?

The biggest wins are usually: storage type (HDD vs SSD), RAM amount, startup programs, and overheating. Diagnostics confirms which one is actually limiting you.

Is an SSD upgrade worth it?

For many older PCs, yes — it’s often the single biggest speed improvement. We can check compatibility and recommend sizes based on your usage.

My PC crashes while gaming — what could it be?

Common causes include overheating, unstable RAM/XMP settings, GPU driver issues, or power delivery problems. We troubleshoot stability first, then performance.

Do you do in-home or business visits?

Yes — depending on the job we can come to you, especially for setup and troubleshooting.
