Why Is My Computer Slow?

A slow computer is often suffering from one or more of the same usual suspects: hard drives, too much startup junk, overheating, not enough RAM, malware, or an aging Windows install.

Answer

The good news is that 'slow' is often fixable. The right fix depends on whether the bottleneck is storage, temperature, background load, or simple age.

Subtronics LLC works with customers all over Genesee County who are trying to make exactly this decision. We focus on what is practical, honest, and likely to give you the best result for your money.
