How Much RAM Do I Need?

The right amount of RAM depends on what you do, not on chasing a number just because it sounds impressive.

Answer

For some users, moderate RAM is plenty. For gaming, content creation, virtualization, and heavy multitasking, more memory can absolutely matter — but only when it matches the workload.

Subtronics LLC works with customers all over Genesee County who are trying to make exactly this decision. We focus on what is practical, honest, and likely to give you the best result for your money.
