When Should I Replace My Computer Instead of Repairing It?

Replacement makes sense when your current machine no longer fits your needs, the repair is poor value, or multiple parts are aging out at once.

Answer

A good repair shop should be willing to tell you when an upgrade or replacement is the smarter move rather than forcing a repair that will disappoint you.

Subtronics LLC works with customers all over Genesee County who are trying to make exactly this decision. We focus on what is practical, honest, and likely to give you the best result for your money.
