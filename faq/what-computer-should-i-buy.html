What Computer Should I Buy?

The best computer to buy depends on your real workload, budget, and how long you want the system to stay useful.

Answer

Some customers only need a reliable mini PC. Others need a gaming tower, workstation, or server-class machine. Matching the hardware to the job matters more than chasing hype.

Subtronics LLC works with customers all over Genesee County who are trying to make exactly this decision. We focus on what is practical, honest, and likely to give you the best result for your money.
