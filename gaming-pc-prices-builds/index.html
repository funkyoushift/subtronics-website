When a prebuilt is the smarter buy

  • You want to stay roughly under $1,200 to $1,400.
  • You want to start gaming sooner.
  • You care more about frame rate per dollar than premium custom extras.
  • You want local setup, file transfer, cleanup, and optimization after purchase.

A good prebuilt plus professional setup is often the best-value answer for budget-conscious buyers.

Ask about a prebuilt

When custom is worth it

  • You want better control over part quality from day one.
  • You care about airflow, thermals, noise, and upgrade path.
  • You want a more tailored parts list and cleaner presentation.
  • You are shopping above basic budget territory.

Custom becomes more compelling when quality, tuning, and long-term flexibility matter more than the cheapest path.
