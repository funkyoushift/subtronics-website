Why customers choose a prebuilt gaming PC

  • Faster path to a working gaming system

Prebuilt vs custom gaming PCs

Prebuilt gaming PCs

Best when you want a working system quickly and would rather spend your time gaming than comparing every last part. They also make sense when you want a cleaner handoff from an old computer to a new one.

Custom gaming PCs

Best when you want exact parts, a specific look, unusual performance targets, or a more tailored long-term upgrade plan. See our custom gaming PC builds page for that option.
