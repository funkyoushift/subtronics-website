Repair, upgrade, or replace?

Some customers need a repair. Some need an upgrade. Some are simply ready for something new. Subtronics LLC helps with all three and explains the tradeoffs honestly so you can choose the option that makes the most sense.

Who this is for

  • People who know they need a new computer but do not know what specs matter
  • Customers choosing between a laptop, mini PC, prebuilt desktop, or custom tower
  • Business owners who need dependable workstations without overspending
  • Power users shopping for creator systems, servers, or high-end workstation hardware

From mini PCs to monster workstations

Subtronics LLC can help whether you need a basic email-and-browsing setup, a gaming rig, a streaming system, or a workstation with serious CPU and memory headroom. We believe the right answer depends on your actual use case — not on selling you the most expensive machine in the room.
