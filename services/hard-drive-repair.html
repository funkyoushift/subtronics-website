Signs your hard drive may be failing

  • Clicking or grinding noises
  • Files taking a long time to open
  • Windows freezing, crashing, or failing to boot
  • Missing files or corrupted folders
  • The system feels much slower than normal for no obvious reason

What we do

We check drive health, confirm whether the problem is the drive itself or something around it, and help you choose the smartest next step. That may be a repair, a replacement drive, an SSD upgrade, or data recovery if the files matter more than the hardware.

Protect your data first

If the drive is failing and your files matter, stop using the computer as much as possible. Continued use can make the recovery odds worse. We’ll tell you honestly whether it makes sense to try recovery first or move straight into a replacement plan.
