Hands-On Learning

Learn to Build Your Own PC

Some people want a finished custom PC. Other people want to learn what goes into one. This page is for the second group.

Not everybody wants a mystery box.
If you want help choosing parts, understanding compatibility, or physically putting everything together, Subtronics LLC can guide the process.
See custom build options

Who this is for

  • People who know what they want but do not want to assemble it alone.
  • People who have no clue what they need and want someone to walk them through it.
  • Parents helping a kid build a first gaming PC.
  • Buyers comparing a mini PC, tower, workstation, or server-style machine.

What you can get help with

Parts Planning

CPU, motherboard, RAM, storage, GPU, power supply, cooling, case size, and realistic budget matching.

Build Guidance

Hands-on help during assembly, cable routing, first boot, BIOS basics, and install checks.

Post-Build Setup

Windows install, driver setup, stress testing, and a clean handoff so the machine is actually ready to use.
