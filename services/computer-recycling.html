Repair, recycle, or replace?

Not every old computer belongs in the recycle pile. Some systems only need an SSD, RAM, cleaning, power supply, Windows repair, or a simple hardware fix.

Diagnostic fee: $65. If we perform diagnostic work and you approve the repair, the full $65 is applied toward the job. If repair is not worth it, we can discuss free recycling for eligible computer equipment.

Free recycling for eligible computer equipment

  • Desktop computers and gaming PCs
  • Laptops and small form factor PCs
  • Motherboards, graphics cards, RAM, CPUs, coolers, and power supplies
  • Storage drives, cables, chargers, keyboards, mice, and computer accessories

TVs, CRT monitors, large appliances, batteries, and non-computer electronics may not be accepted or may require a separate disposal option.

How it works

  1. Call or text 810-280-2222 with the device type, symptoms, and photos if available.
  2. We help decide whether a phone consult, on-site diagnostic, or recycling conversation makes sense.
  3. If the system may be repairable, we schedule diagnostic service.
  4. If repair is not worth it, eligible computer equipment can be recycled at no additional recycling charge.

Why Subtronics handles it this way

Free recycling is meant to keep useful computer hardware out of the landfill while giving each computer one last chance to be repaired or reused.

That means we do not advertise a public drop-off address, do not ask customers to haul equipment across town blindly, and do not promise that every item has resale or repair value. We start with an honest conversation, then recommend the most practical next step.
