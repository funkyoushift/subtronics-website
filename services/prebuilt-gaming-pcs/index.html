Prebuilt Gaming PCs

This page exists for people searching specifically for prebuilts, but the main buying page is still Gaming PCs for Every Budget so budget and custom buyers land in the same place.

Best fit for most prebuilt shoppers:
Tier 1 systems usually fall around $800–$1,400 depending on current deals and goals.
Local setup, cleanup, updates, and optional file transfer are available.

Why start with a prebuilt?

  • Lower entry price
  • Faster turnaround
  • Good for tighter budgets
  • Easy path when you just want to play without overthinking every part

When custom makes more sense

  • You want better long-term upgrade room
  • You care about PSU, airflow, motherboard, and case quality
  • You want a more tailored part list
  • You are already above the budget sweet spot for value prebuilts

Use the main combined page to compare both options

We keep Tier 1 prebuilts and custom builds together there on purpose so pricing is easier to understand.
