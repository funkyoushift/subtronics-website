Why Your Computer Is Running Slow

Why computers slow down and what to do first. Subtronics LLC provides computer diagnostics, repairs, and upgrades in Flint and Flushing, Michigan.

Common Causes

  • Too many startup programs
  • Low storage space
  • Malware or unwanted programs
  • Old hard drive (HDD) instead of SSD
  • Overheating or dust buildup

Things You Can Try First

  • Restart your computer
  • Disable unnecessary startup apps
  • Uninstall programs you don’t use
  • Run a malware scan
  • Free up disk space

When a Repair Technician Is Needed

If the problem continues after basic troubleshooting, it may need professional diagnostics and repair:

  • SSD upgrade
  • Virus removal / cleanup
  • Hardware diagnostics
  • Thermal cleanup and cooling improvements
