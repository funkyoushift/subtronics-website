What to Do If Your Computer Won’t Turn On

Troubleshooting steps for a PC that won’t power on. Learn what to check and when to call a technician in Genesee County, Michigan.

Common Causes

  • Loose power cable or dead power strip
  • Failed power supply (desktop) or charger (laptop)
  • Battery or charging port issues
  • Bad RAM seating
  • Motherboard failure

Things You Can Try First

  • Try a different wall outlet / power strip
  • Check the power cable is fully seated
  • Hold the power button for 10 seconds, then try again
  • Remove all USB devices and retry
  • If comfortable: reseat RAM (desktop) or try one stick at a time

When a Repair Technician Is Needed

If the problem continues after basic troubleshooting, it may need professional diagnostics and repair:

  • Power supply testing and replacement
  • DC jack / charging port repair
  • Motherboard-level diagnostics
  • Data recovery if the drive is failing
