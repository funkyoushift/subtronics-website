Computer Repair FAQ

How much is your diagnostic fee, and is it applied to the repair?

Our diagnostic fee is $65. If you move forward with the repair, that fee is applied toward the total cost.

Do you repair slow computers and random shutdown issues?

Yes. Slow performance and random shutdowns are two of the most common problems we fix. These issues are often caused by failing hardware, overheating, storage problems, or software conflicts, and we diagnose the actual cause before recommending a repair.

Do you offer data recovery services?

Yes. We can recover data from failing or damaged drives in many cases. If your files are important, stop using the computer and contact us right away so the problem does not get worse.

Do you work on custom gaming PCs and hardware upgrades?

Yes. We repair, upgrade, and build custom gaming PCs. Whether you need better performance, new parts installed, or help planning a build, we can walk you through the best options for your budget.

Do you remove viruses and clean up infected computers?

Yes. We remove viruses, malware, browser hijackers, and unwanted programs, then make sure your system is clean and running properly again.

Do you offer remote support if I can’t bring my computer in?

Yes. In many cases we can diagnose and fix issues remotely. If the problem needs hands-on work, we also offer local service options in Flushing, Flint, Grand Blanc, and surrounding Genesee County areas.
