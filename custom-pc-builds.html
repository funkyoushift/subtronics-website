You do not need to visit a different page to compare prebuilts vs custom. The point of this page is to keep that decision simple.

Which path makes the most sense?

  • Choose Tier 1 prebuilt if your main goal is value, speed, and staying under roughly $1,200–$1,400.
  • Choose starter custom if you want a better motherboard, PSU, airflow, case, and upgrade path from day one.
  • Move into performance or above if you care about high refresh 1440p, 4K, streaming, creator work, or premium thermals.
