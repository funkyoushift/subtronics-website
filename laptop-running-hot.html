Why Your Laptop Is Running Hot

Laptop overheating guide: symptoms, quick fixes, and when it needs cleaning, thermal paste, or fan service.

Common Causes

  • Dust in heatsink and fan
  • Worn thermal paste
  • Background apps using CPU
  • Blocked vents or poor placement
  • Failing fan

Things You Can Try First

  • Use the laptop on a hard surface
  • Check Task Manager for high usage
  • Lower performance mode temporarily
  • Clean vents gently (do not force dust deeper)
  • Update BIOS/drivers if available

When a Repair Technician Is Needed

If the problem continues after basic troubleshooting, it may need professional diagnostics and repair:

  • Deep internal cleaning
  • Thermal paste replacement
  • Fan replacement
  • Thermal diagnostics and stability testing
