FAQ

Can you keep my files and programs?

Often yes — we can clone the old drive to the new SSD if the old drive is healthy enough.

What if my old hard drive is failing?

We can attempt a safer backup or a partial clone and prioritize your important data.

Do SSD upgrades help gaming?

They improve load times and overall system responsiveness. FPS gains are usually from CPU/GPU, not storage.
