Computer Overheating: What to Do

Overheating can cause slowdowns, loud fans, stuttering, and shutdowns. Most overheating is dust buildup or failing cooling parts.

Start with the safe steps below. If you’re uncomfortable opening anything, we can handle it.

Common signs

  • Fans constantly loud
  • System slows down under load
  • Random shutdowns
  • Laptop gets very hot

Safe things to try

  1. Use a hard, ventilated surface.
  2. Check Task Manager for high usage while idle.
  3. Clean dust from visible vents carefully.

When it needs service

  • Overheats again quickly
  • Shuts off under load
  • Fan rattling or not spinning

We can deep clean, replace thermal paste, and verify temps under load.

FAQ

Is overheating dangerous?

Yes. Heat can throttle performance and shorten component lifespan.

Why does it overheat on a couch or bed?

Soft surfaces block vents and trap heat.
