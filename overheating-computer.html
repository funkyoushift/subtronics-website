Why Your Computer Is Overheating

Fix overheating desktops and laptops. Learn symptoms, causes, and safe steps you can take before scheduling a repair.

Common Causes

  • Dust buildup blocking airflow
  • Dry or improperly applied thermal paste
  • Failing fan or pump (AIO coolers)
  • Poor airflow or cramped placement
  • High background CPU/GPU usage

Things You Can Try First

  • Shut down and let it cool
  • Make sure vents are not blocked
  • Check Task Manager for high usage
  • Clean visible dust with compressed air (carefully)
  • Update GPU drivers and BIOS if applicable

When a Repair Technician Is Needed

If the problem continues after basic troubleshooting, it may need professional diagnostics and repair:

  • Thermal paste replacement
  • Deep dust cleaning and fan service
  • CPU/GPU temperature diagnostics
  • Cooler replacement and airflow optimization
