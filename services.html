  1. Reach out (call/text) and tell us what’s happening.
  2. We diagnose the issue and explain your options.
  3. You approve the plan — then we repair, upgrade, or build it.

We don’t push parts you don’t need. If an upgrade makes sense, we’ll explain why. If it doesn’t, we’ll tell you that too.
