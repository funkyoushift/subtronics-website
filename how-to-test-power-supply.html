Tools You May Need

  • Phillips screwdriver
  • Anti-static precautions
  • Clean workspace

General Steps

  1. Turn off and unplug your computer.
  2. Open the computer case or access panel.
  3. Locate the component being replaced or tested.
  4. Carefully remove the component.
  5. Install or test the replacement hardware.
  6. Reassemble the system and power it back on.

Common Problems

  • Computer won't boot after upgrade
  • No display after installing hardware
  • Drivers not recognized
