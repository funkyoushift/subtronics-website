What is TechCare?

TechCare is a membership program designed to keep your computer running fast and secure without surprise repair bills. If something breaks or starts acting weird, we’ll help you remotely when possible, or get you scheduled quickly for in-shop service.

FAQ

Is remote support safe?

Yes. Remote support requires your permission and can be ended at any time by closing the tool.

Do you include hardware parts?

No. Parts are not included. Members receive discounted labor on installs and repairs.

Does TechCare include drive cloning?

Drive cloning is available at a member discount. If you need an SSD upgrade or drive replacement, we’ll quote the best option for your system.

Can I cancel?

Yes. Monthly plans can be canceled anytime. Annual plans are billed yearly and typically non-refundable once service has been used.
