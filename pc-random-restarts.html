PC Randomly Restarting? Here’s What to Check

Random restarts are often caused by overheating, unstable power, or driver crashes. Don’t guess parts—use the checklist to narrow it down.

Note whether it happens at idle, during gaming, or on boot.

Common causes

  • Overheating CPU/GPU
  • Power supply instability
  • Driver crashes
  • RAM errors

Checklist

  1. Check temps and fan behavior.
  2. Update Windows and drivers.
  3. Run memory diagnostic.
  4. Check Event Viewer for critical errors.

When to call us

  • Restarts repeat under load
  • Black screen restarts
  • Heat + restarts together

We’ll stress test and identify the real cause.

FAQ

Why does my PC restart while gaming?

Gaming loads can reveal overheating, PSU weakness, or GPU driver crashes.

Can bad RAM cause random restarts?

Yes. Memory instability can cause crashes and reboots.
