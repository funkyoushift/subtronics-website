Good DIY jobs vs. stop-and-call jobs

Good DIY jobs

Cleaning dust, swapping RAM, adding storage, learning your parts list, and following a beginner-friendly full build guide.

Stop-and-call jobs

Liquid damage, burning smells, repeated no-boot symptoms, risky data recovery, power supply concerns, and anything that makes you feel unsure.
