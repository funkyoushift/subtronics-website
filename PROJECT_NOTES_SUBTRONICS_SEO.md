# SEO Philosophy

The site aims to become a **local computer repair authority site** for Genesee County.

Primary strategy:

- Build topical depth around real customer problems
- Capture local intent with city pages
- Capture research intent with help / FAQ pages
- Capture trust with DIY tutorials and right-to-repair positioning
- Convert readers into leads with soft, honest calls to action

The tone should stay:

- knowledgeable
- trustworthy
- local
- not pushy
- pro-DIY without scaring away service customers
