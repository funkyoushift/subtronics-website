Common causes of a slow PC

  • Old hard drive (HDD) — upgrading to an SSD is often the biggest improvement
  • Not enough RAM for what you do
  • Too many startup programs / background apps
  • Overheating (dust, failing fans, old thermal paste)
  • Storage nearly full
  • Malware/adware (we can help diagnose and clean up)
