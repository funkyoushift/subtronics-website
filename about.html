About Subtronics LLC

Subtronics LLC is a local computer repair and custom PC business based in Flushing, Michigan, built around practical help, honest advice, and real one-on-one service.

Hi — I’m Martin.

I run Subtronics LLC and I’m usually the person you’re talking to — and the person doing the work. Most people call me when their computer is slow, crashing, overheating, or they’re not sure what upgrade actually makes sense.

My goal is simple: fix the problem without wasting your money. If a repair is worth it, I’ll tell you why. If it’s not, I’ll tell you that too.
