About Subtronics LLC

Subtronics LLC is a locally owned computer repair and custom PC business based in Flushing, Michigan. We help people and small businesses across Genesee County with repairs, upgrades, troubleshooting, and builds.

What we stand for

  • Honest diagnostics: understand the real problem before buying parts.
  • Clear pricing: parts and labor are explained up front.
  • Quality work: stability, airflow, thermals, and clean cable management.
  • Support: you can talk directly to the person doing the work.

Meet the owner

I’m Martin. I build and repair PCs for gaming, streaming, school, and professional work — and I’m obsessed with solving the “weird problems” that other shops rush past.
